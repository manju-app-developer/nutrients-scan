Information We Collect

Nutri Scan is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect personally identifiable information.

  • Image Data: When you use our service to analyze a food item, the image you capture is sent to a secure backend service for processing by an AI model. This image is used solely for the purpose of identifying the food and is not stored or logged on our servers after the analysis is complete.
  • Scan History: To provide the "Scan History" feature, the results of your analysis (food names and portion sizes) are stored locally in your web browser's storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers and remains exclusively on your device. Clearing your browser's cache or using the "Clear History" button will permanently delete this data.
  • Analytics: We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the service. This may include information like your device type, browser, and general location (e.g., country). This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Third-Party Services (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.
